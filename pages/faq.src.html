Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS VANIX STUDIO?

+

VANIX STUDIO is a next-generation AI cinematic production company that merges cutting-edge artificial intelligence with the art of storytelling. We produce films, VFX, web series, storyboards, and commercial content using proprietary AI pipelines.

HOW LONG DOES A PROJECT TAKE?

+

Project timelines vary based on scope. A short-form commercial or teaser can be produced in 1–2 weeks. Full-scale AI films or web series episodes typically take 4–8 weeks from concept to delivery. Contact us for a personalized timeline estimate.

WHAT FORMATS DO YOU DELIVER IN?

+

We deliver in all standard broadcast and streaming formats including 4K UHD (H.264/H.265), ProRes 4444, and custom formats on request. Source files and project archives are available for enterprise clients.

DO YOU OFFER REVISION ROUNDS?

+

Yes. All packages include structured revision rounds at key milestones — concept approval, storyboard review, rough cut, and final delivery. Additional revisions beyond the agreed scope are billed at our hourly rate.

HOW DO I START A PROJECT?

+

Simply reach out via our Contact page with your project brief, timeline, and budget. Our team will respond within 24 hours with a consultation appointment and initial proposal.

IS MY CONTENT KEPT CONFIDENTIAL?

+

Absolutely. All client projects are protected under NDA by default. We never share, publish, or reference client content without explicit written permission. Enterprise clients receive dedicated isolated project environments.
