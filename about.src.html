OUR STORY

Born From The Intersection of AI & Cinema

VANIX STUDIO was founded with a singular vision: to harness the transformative power of artificial intelligence and reshape the entire landscape of cinematic production.

We believe that AI is not replacing human creativity — it's amplifying it. Our team of visionary filmmakers, AI engineers, and creative technologists work together to produce content that was once thought impossible.

From our state-of-the-art AI production facility, we've delivered over 150 projects spanning films, web series, commercials, VFX showcases, and immersive visual experiences for clients worldwide.
